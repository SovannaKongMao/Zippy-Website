Our Story

How did we startup Zippy?

It all starts with one main issue that Cambodian parcel delivery companies are failing to do. It is to provide a pleasant experience for both the customer and the seller. We introduced this idea and pitched it to the NICC Bandos Incubation Program. After one month of training, we successfully pitched and managed to get $10,000 in Seed Funding for our company.

Frequently Asked Questions

Want to know something about Zippy Express?

How to Start using ZippyExpress?

Go to Play Store or App Store and search for Zippy Express, then download, sign up, and agree to the terms and conditions before booking and using our delivery service. ( App is coming soon )

What is the charging fee of Zippy Express?

Zippy Express simply charges for the distance between your location and the recipient's location; it does not charge for pick-up services, returning items, or collecting payment from customers.

Do my customers need to download the App to fill their information?

Our system for customer uses is going to be based on a Web-App, meaning they won't need to download any app, just open the form and fill in with their browser.

What is item is prohibited when using Zippy Express?

Zippy Express is a legitimate corporation in Cambodia that is registered with the Ministry of Commerce and the General Department of Taxation. Addictive drugs, firearms, explosives, and all other products that are hazardous, unlawful, and in violation of Cambodian law are strictly prohibited.
